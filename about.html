What is Biobuddy?

A friend is all what we need. Biobuddy is a organization website that aims to encourage the populace to treat anything that deals with life as a friend through friendly means.

What is its goal?

It seeks to propagate the green movement to encourage the people to abandon their destructive attitude towards nature and the organisms in it, thus, protecting and preserving life to a maximal state.

Why Biobuddy?

Biobuddy is among the leading organizations that provides awareness towards anything that deals with life. It provides updates and latest info about the current happenings and stand of the environment from the floras and faunas to the Earth itself.
